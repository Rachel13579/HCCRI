United Nations Environment Programme (UNEP)

TOPIC 1: Accelerating the Process of Worldwide Decarbonisation

TOPIC 2: Mitigation of Natural Disasters

Who is the climate, and why are they changing? Delve into the complex geopolitics and myriad barriers impeding the progress of worldwide decarbonisation and mitigation of natural disasters. As a UNEP delegate, you will be tasked to contend with the capricious tendencies of Mother Nature — and your fellow delegates — in devising solutions to slow down runaway climate change, and minimise the loss of life and property in an increasingly inhospitable world.

European Union (EU)

TOPIC 1: Reforming Europe’s Refugee Legislation, Immigration Policy and Border Control

TOPIC 2: Diversification and Greenification of the EU’s Energy Sources

"They say when trouble comes, close ranks," but that is not what the EU, as the pillar of international human rights and defender of liberal ideals, can or should do. Amidst both external and internal strife, EU delegates should strike a balance between defending its ideals and protecting its regional interests carefully. You will be challenged to handle the issue of accommodating refugees whilst maintaining national unity, diverting resources and reforming immigration systems to better facilitate the rights of those who wash up on the EU's shores. This increased population will boost the continent’s energy demand. Given the EU’s current energy crisis, delegates will power through debate regarding the highly pertinent diversification and greenification of sources. Hopefully, they will be blown away by their experience in council!
